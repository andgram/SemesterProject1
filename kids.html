Innovation Station: Where Creativity Meets Science

Elevate your scientific exploration at the Innovation Station, a dedicated space where creativity and experimentation converge. From constructing machines to delving into chemistry, this hands-on hub empowers young minds to unleash their creativity. Join us at the Innovation Station, where your ideas take center stage, and innovation knows no limits.

Becoming a Junior Scientist: Hands-On Research Adventures

Take your passion for discovery to new heights by joining our Junior Scientist program. Designed for aspiring researchers, this program offers a unique opportunity to work alongside our scientists on real-world projects. Dive into the world of scientific inquiry, from studying animal behavior to unraveling the mysteries of space. Become a Junior Scientist and actively contribute to the fascinating world of ongoing research.

We invite you to delve into the diverse realms of science at our museum, where each experience is an invitation to learn, explore, and be inspired.
